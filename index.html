Los Angeles Lakers

History

Founded in 1947, as of now Lakers are the most famous and successfull franchise.

Since summer 2012, Lakers have the most wins (3,125), winning percentage (.625) and NBA Final appearances (31).

Lakers are second in NBA Champions (1) behind the Boston Celtics.
